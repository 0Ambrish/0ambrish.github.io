About CM.com

CM.com (AMS: CMCOM) is a global leader in cloud software for conversational commerce, enabling businesses to deliver superior customer experiences. Our communications and payments platform empowers marketing, sales, and customer support to automate engagement with customers across multiple mobile channels, blended with seamless payment capabilities that drive sales, gain customers, and increase customer happiness.

Founded in 1999 and headquartered in Breda, Netherlands, CM.com operates in over 190 countries, serving more than 25,000 customers with a team of over 300 employees.

Our Platforms

  • Engagement Platform: Connects data and technology for seamless, personalized communication across the entire customer journey.
  • Connectivity Platform: Builds customer connections on their preferred communication channels via one platform.
  • Payments Platform: Provides all you need to accept transactions; online and in-person payments on one platform.
  • Ticketing Platform: Amplifies the visitor experience with one integrated platform.
